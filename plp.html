    This fabulous collection is a firm favourite with our design team because it brings rustic charm without the upkeep. These terracotta effect tiles capture the warmth and texture of traditional terracotta, but with none of the sealing fuss. This makes them the perfect tiles to live with.

    These tiles are lovely in a country cottage, striking in a retro setting, and full of Mediterranean soul when paired with a décor tile like those from our Marrakesh collection. Our terracotta effect tiles are available in both glazed ceramic - which is very easy to clean - and matt porcelain - a hard-wearing material that is perfect for higher traffic areas.
